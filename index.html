Mammals and Fish of the Salish Sea

The Salish sea is a body of salt water and has Orcas

Orcas are sometimes called Killer whales

Here are some other Salish Sea facts provided by the SeaDoc Socitey:

  1. There are 419 islands in the Salish Sea area.
  2. Many marine creatures call the Salish Sea home.
  3. There is at least one pod of Orcas that travels throught the area.

There are even some endangered creatures living in the Salish Sea.

  • Several species of salmon migrate through the Salish Sea.
  • Marbled murrelets, a type of endagered sea bird also rely on this ocean habitat.
Many people are involved in protecting and educating the people in Washington about marine life as well as other related topics. For moree detailed resources you can view the UW Campus Library webpages.
